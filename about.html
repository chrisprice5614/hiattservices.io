About

Always Serving

At Hiatt Services, we are a family-owned business proudly serving the greater Spokane area since 2012. We specialize in hazardous tree removal, retaining walls, logging, land clearing, asphalt and concrete demolition, backfilling, compacting, and building demolition. Our dedication to delivering high-quality work at competitive prices has earned us the trust of our community. In addition to our primary services, we offer a wide range of solutions for both residential and commercial clients. From tree removal, pruning, and fire reduction to road and driveway construction, site preparation, and dump truck services, we handle it all. Led by our owner, Chet Hiatt, we are fully licensed, bonded, and insured, ensuring every project we undertake is completed with professionalism and care.
